Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2026

WinnPro Shows ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile information you choose to provide.
  • Business Card Information: Information you add to your digital business card, including name, photo, contact details, and interests.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including vendors you save and interactions within the app.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers for push notifications.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Enable you to share your digital business card with vendors
  • Allow vendors to capture and manage leads
  • Send push notifications about the event (with your consent)
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Communicate with you about your account or our services

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Vendors: When you share your business card with a vendor, they receive the information on your card.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services (such as Firebase and Stripe) to operate our app.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database, and file storage
  • Google Sign-In: For account authentication
  • Apple Sign-In: For account authentication on iOS devices
  • Biometric Authentication: Face ID, Touch ID, or device biometrics for quick sign-in (stored locally on your device only)
  • Stripe: For payment processing (vendor features)

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Biometric Data

When you enable biometric login (Face ID, Touch ID, or device biometrics), your biometric data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. We only store a reference identifier locally on your device to enable quick sign-in.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
